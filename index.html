a wide open door

You have been invited to our Apartment Exposition. This event will be held on Saturday May 24th from 5-9 p.m. The location is 3802 Rue Sewell. We will be sharing the work of Syd Henry Lewis, Lore Schwartz, Kate Sianos, and Katariina Paavola. Wine tastings sourced from the Greater Montreal area and small bites will be available on a first-come-first-serve basis. Live music be performed throughout the evening including piano by Audrey Bramlett and a DJ set by Arnaud.

Please avoid sharing this event over social media - if you know anyone that might be interested in our little gathering, let them know next time you see them, or send them the poster and the link to this website. To all who want art, wine, food, music, prosperity, a good harvest, and a lucky lotto ticket - we welcome you in peace.
